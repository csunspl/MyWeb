Introduction

Ye Zhu received his Ph.D degree from Texas A&M University. Currently he is an professor in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department, Cleveland State University. Before joining Cleveland State University, he was a research assistant in the NetCamo research group. His industry experience includes summer internship at Microsoft and full-time work in Optical Networking Group (ONG) in Lucent Technologies. His current research focuses on cyberphysical systems, network security and privacy, privacy-preserving data mining, and security and privacy issues in AI/deep learning.
